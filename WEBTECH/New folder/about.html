ABOUT US

Welcome to Late Night Cafe, your go-to place for delicious meals after dark. We're open every night from 8PM to 3AM for all IIUM students looking for food, chill vibes, or a quiet place to study.

We keep things simple, tasty, and affordable. Our menu is designed for students — no fancy prices, just real food for real nights.

Transparency matters. That’s why our menu shows nutrition info, including calories, sugar, protein, ingredients, and allergens. You can even customize your meals online and check the details instantly.

Whether you're finishing a last-minute assignment, hanging with friends, or just need a midnight snack, we’ve got your back with fresh food, fast service, and student-friendly prices.
